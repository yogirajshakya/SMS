Snapshots

Snapshots are a powerful new feature that allows users to save all settings for a given panel and reuse them later with just one click - it is as easy as taking a snapshot! Whether you are setting your delivery options, or configuring the way a file should be imported, never reconfigure anything from scratch again - save a snapshot, reload it later, and you're done.

Snapshots extend the concept of "profiles" to a new level: almost every group of related settings in SendBlaster can be saved as a snapshot. Look for Take snapshot and Load snapshot buttons in the upper right part of most panels.
