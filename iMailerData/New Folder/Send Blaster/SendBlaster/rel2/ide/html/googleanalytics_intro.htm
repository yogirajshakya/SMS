What is Google Analytics?

Google Analytics is the most widespread web analytics solution that gives you rich insights into your website traffic and marketing effectiveness. If you already use Analytics on your website, you will be able to track your e-mail campaigns too: just select the "Google" button in SendBlaster's message editing panel - nothing else to configure!

Please be aware that, while SendBlaster has full integration with the service, Google Analytics is not part of SendBlaster (the software) itself.

Did your customers buy any product after your email campaign?

Google Analytics gives you easy ways to track what happens after someone clicks a link in your email newsletter and lands on your site:

  • Conversion tracking - Discover if you reached your goals (sell products, get new subscriptions, etc)
  • Compare email campaigns with other sources (ads, organic search)
  • Get clue information about campaign effectiveness:
    - average number of visited pages
    - average time on your website
    - users immediately leaving your site
    - and much more useful information...
