Scanning

The Wizard will now scan your PC searching for available ports, try to connect to the SMTP server and send a test message. This may take some minutes, depending on how fast an available port is found.

If you are running a security software (firewall / antivirus), it may ask you if you want to allow SendBlaster to access the Internet; if you are asked, please answer "yes" or "allow" in order to grant SendBlaster full access to the Internet. We also suggest that your antivirus outgoing mail scan is temporarily disabled.

In the meanwhile, you may want to have a look at turboSMTP dedicated services exclusively designed for SendBlaster's users.
